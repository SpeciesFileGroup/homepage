Current

To join any of the Zoom meetings you will need a link to the Zoom room. There are two ways to get the link:

  • Be sent the Zoom link via email as a reminder before the meeting (typically 1-2 days before). To do so, subscribe to this email list: https://lists.illinois.edu/lists/info/sfg-announce.
  • Chat with a member of the Species File Group and its collaborators at https://gitter.im/SpeciesFileGroup/taxonworks.
We do not post the links here to prevent Zoom bombing.

Weekly Digitization and TaxonWorks Zoom

  • What: Help grow a community of digitization expertise around TaxonWorks. A 1-2 hr weekly open question and answer session with a focus on natural history specimen digitization using TaxonWorks. The agenda is set by those with specific questions. Updates to TaxonWorks are briefly covered. Design and discussion of new functionality frequently happens.
  • When: Wednesdays, weekly. 13:30 CDT (18:30 UTC)
  • Where: Zoom.
  • Who: Open to anyone.
  • Add to calendar (ICS file).

Nomenclature and TaxonWorks Zoom

  • What: TaxonWorks excels at digitizing nomenclature and its metadata. The agenda is set by users with specific questions and use cases. In addition we highlight new functionality related to digitization of nomenclature.
  • When: Wednesdays, alternating other week (currently March 17, 24, etc.). 10:30 CDT (15:30 UTC)
  • Where: Zoom.
  • Who: Open to anyone.
  • How: To join, find a current participant or use the Gitter channel and they will pass on the Zoom link. We do not post the link here to prevent Zoom bombing.
  • Note: A companion set of sessions focused on the curation of the Universal Chalcidoidea Database happens at the same time on alternate weeks. While slightly more focused, many non-hymenopterists frequent these sessions. If interested reach out to us for details!
  • Add to calendar (ICS file).
